VNIT Student Portal helps students with their Job/Internship Interview Preparation for On-Off Campus recruitements. We provide Interview Experiences, Tips and other contact information of students who have already been interviewed at various companies.



Why we think Interview Experiences solves a problem ?

What we expect from you ?

Experiences are powerful. We expect you to share your interview experience, as it can help students prepare for their interviews.



VISION

Our vision is to help students in VNIT prepare for their Job/Internship Interviews with some beforehand tips and preparatory questions contributed by their seniors and batchmates.
